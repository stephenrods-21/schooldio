Students

Allows you to manage Student model.

Fetches a list of all students.[GET, POST]
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/students/

Get a student detail.[GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE]
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/students/{pk}
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/students/1

Fetches a list of all students those belonging to a particular school.[GET, POST]
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/schools/{school_pk}/students
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/schools/1/students

Get a student detail belonging to a particular school.[GET, PUT, PATCH, DELETE]
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/schools/{school_pk}/students/{pk}
https://schooldio-django.herokuapp.com/api/schools/1/students/2
